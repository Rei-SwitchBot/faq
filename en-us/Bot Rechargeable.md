# Bot Rechargeable FAQ

---
### Can the Rechargeable Battery of the Rechargeable Bot Be Used in the Regular Bot?

The regular Bot does not support rechargeable batteries, so using them may cause incorrect battery level readings or prevent the switch from being pressed properly. Therefore, do not use Rechargeable Bot rechargeable batteries with regular Bot.

---
### Which Type of Charging Adapter Do I Need to Use for the Rechargeable Battery of SwitchBot Bot Rechargeable?

Please use a DC charging adapter with a 5V 1A output to charge the Bot Rechargeable battery.
