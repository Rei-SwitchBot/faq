### How Does SwitchBot AI MindClip Protect User Privacy and Data Security?

SwitchBot AI MindClip protects your privacy and data across the device, app, and cloud.
Data Storage
Recordings are stored on the MindClip device, in the app on your phone, and in your personal cloud storage account.
Transcripts, summaries, and AskAI data are stored in the app on your phone and in your personal cloud storage account.
Device-Level Privacy Protection
After recordings are transferred from the device to the app and cloud, the local recording files on the device are automatically cleared.
If the device is lost, other people cannot add or use it. MindClip also does not provide any data export port. The Type-C port is used for charging only.
When you unbind the device, all recordings that have not yet been transferred can be completely deleted to help prevent data leakage.
App-Level Privacy Protection
All data in the app is stored locally on your own phone.
Cloud-Level Privacy Protection
Cloud data is stored in your personal cloud storage account, and only you have access to it.
Your personal cloud data is hosted on Amazon Web Services, with strict access controls in place.
If you delete your account, your personal cloud data can also be completely removed.
MindClip has passed strict privacy and data security certifications, including EN18031, ISO27001, and ISO27701.
SwitchBot does not use your data for AI model training.

### Is Recording Data Uploaded to a Public Server?

No. Recording data is stored in the users personal cloud account, and only the user has access to it. When the account is deleted, the personal cloud data can also be fully removed. User data is not used for AI model training.

### Can Someone Else Use SwitchBot AI MindClip or Export Data If It Is Lost?

No. If the device is lost, another person cannot add or use it. The Type-C port is for charging only and does not provide any data export interface. Users can also use Find My to locate the device.
