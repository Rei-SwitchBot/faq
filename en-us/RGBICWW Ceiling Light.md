## RGBICWW Ceiling Light

---

---
### How to Reset SwitchBot RGBICWW Ceiling Light

Turn the RGBICWW Ceiling Light on and off five times in a row, with each interval within 10 seconds. Once the device flashes red, green, and blue in sequence, it has been restored to factory settings. After the factory reset, the Wi-Fi information and device settings will be cleared.

---
### Can Multiple RGBICWW Ceiling Lights Be Used as a Group?

We regret to inform you that RGBICWW Ceiling Light does not support group control at this time. If you would like to control multiple lights together, you can use SwitchBot Scenes or Automation as an alternative. By adding multiple RGBICWW Ceiling Lights as actions in the same Scene or Automation, you can have them respond to the same command.
Please note that due to network conditions, there may be a slight delay when multiple RGBICWW Ceiling Lights execute the same command. As a result, the lights may not turn on or off at exactly the same time.

---
### Can RGBICWW Ceiling Light Display Different Colors in Different Areas?

We regret to inform you that RGBICWW Ceiling Light does not support zone-based color control at this time.
The light can display a selected static color, such as red, blue, or green, but the entire light panel will show the same color. It does not support setting different areas of the light panel to different colors at the same time, such as having one area in red, another in blue, and another in green.

---
### How to Add SwitchBot RGBICWW Ceiling Light to SwitchBot App

1. Make sure your SwitchBot App version is up to date.

2. Add your RGBICWW Ceiling Light to SwitchBot App in the following order:
- Launch SwitchBot App and log in.
- Tap "+" icon in the upper right corner.
- Tap "Add Device".
- Scroll down and tap "RGBICWW Ceiling Light" in the "Lighting" section.
- Please turn on the RGBICWW Ceiling Light.
- Tap "Connect Device" to connect the device.
- Once the connection is complete, enter the SSID and password for the 2.4GHz Wi-Fi.
- Tap "Start Using" to finish pairing.

Note:
Please use the latest version of the SwitchBot app from the app store. You may not be able to pair with the beta version.
Please complete pairing within 2 minutes after the Ceiling Light is connected to power. After 120 seconds, you will need to power the device off and on again.

---
### Does RGBICWW Ceiling Light Have Hub Functionality?

No. RGBICWW Ceiling Light does not have Hub functionality and cannot connect SwitchBot Bluetooth devices to the internet. If you need this function, please purchase a SwitchBot Hub product.

---
### Can I Add Infrared Remote Controls Using RGBICWW Ceiling Light?

We regret to inform you that RGBICWW Ceiling Light does not support infrared remote learning. If you need to add and control infrared remote controls, please consider purchasing a SwitchBot Hub product, such as Hub Mini.
