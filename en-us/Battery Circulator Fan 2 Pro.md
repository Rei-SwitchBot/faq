### How to Put SwitchBot Battery Circulator Fan 2 Pro into Matter Pairing Mode

**Method 1 Add SwitchBot Battery Circulator Fan 2 Pro to Apple Home without Using SwitchBot App**
Press and hold the power button on the Battery Circulator Fan 2 Pro control panel for 15 seconds. The device will reset and then enter pairing mode for Matter.
Open the Apple Home app, tap "Add or Scan Accessory," and scan the QR code on the device to add it to the Apple Home app.

**Method 2 Add SwitchBot Battery Circulator Fan 2 Pro to Apple Home with SwitchBot App**
Open Apple Home App -> Tap "Add or scan Accessory" in the upper right corner -> Tap "More options" -> Tap "My Accessory isn't shown here" at the bottom of the screen -> Tap "Enter Code" -> Paste the code to continue the whole process.
You can check the Matter setup code on the device itself or via the SwitchBot app by navigating to Battery Circulator Fan 2 Pro→ Gear Icon → Matter Setup.
