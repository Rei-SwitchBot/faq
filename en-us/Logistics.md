### When Will the Product I Purchased Be Shipped?

1. Your order might be held to ship all at once if it contains a pre-order item. We kindly ask for a little more time for these deliveries to be processed. If you're in a hurry for the items currently in stock, please contact our support team and we can look into shipping those for you first.
2. If your order is marked as "Fulfilled" even if only part of your shipment has been sent, please rest assured that the remaining products in your order will be shipped shortly. This status update is part of the processing system, and we'll make sure everything you purchased reaches you.
3. You can track the delivery progress of your order using the logistics provider listed in the shipping details.
4. For more information about our shipping policy, please refer to the link below.
https://www.switch-bot.com/pages/switchbot-shipping-policy
5. If you have any further questions, please provide your order number, and our service team will check the details for you shortly.
