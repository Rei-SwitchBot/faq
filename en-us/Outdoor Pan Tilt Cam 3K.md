### Is SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K Waterproof?

SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K features IP66-rated waterproofing for outdoor use. However, during heavy rain or snow, a protective case is recommended to prevent damage to the device from prolonged exposure to the elements.
The following describes the operating environment of the Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K.
Temperature: -20 °C to 45 °C (-4 °F～113 °F)
Humidity: ≤95%

### How Long Is SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K Charging Cable?

SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K comes with a 3-meter charging cable, and the specifications for the charging adapter are DC 12V 2A.

### What Is the Type C Port of SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K Used for?

The Type-C interface of the outdoor camera provides 5V1A output, which can power the outdoor fill light.

### What Type of Network Cable Should I Use for SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K?

SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K can connect through both Wi-Fi and wired LAN. If you prefer a more stable connection, consider using a wired LAN instead of relying solely on your home Wi-Fi. The LAN port on the Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K features an RJ-45 connector, and you can use various twisted pair cables, ranging from Cat5 to Cat8, without restrictions. However, please note that the transmission speed may vary based on the specifications of the cable and the performance of the connected devices. RJ-45 is commonly used in networks supporting speeds of 100Mbps or 1Gbps, typically connecting with cables like Cat5e, Cat6, Cat6A, Cat7, or Cat8.

### What Should Be Considered During Installation to Ensure Smooth Viewing of SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K's Video?

1. Install the Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K within 20 meters of the router, ensuring that no more than two walls separate the camera from the router.
2. Please mount the camera at a height of 2.5 to 3 meters above the ground.

### Troubleshooting SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K Fails to Connect to Wi-Fi and Cannot Be Added to the SwitchBot App

Thank you for reaching out to us. We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused and appreciate the opportunity to assist you.
Regarding this issue, could you please check the following points?
1. Please use the latest version of the SwitchBot app from the app store. You may not be able to pair with the beta version.
2. If your home Wi-Fi router supports both 5GHz and 2.4GHz frequencies, please connect to the 2.4GHz network. If your Wi-Fi router is set to use the 2.4GHz band for 802.11 b/g/n and has ax enabled, please disable ax.
3. When adding the device, make sure to correctly enter the Wi-Fi SSID and password, paying attention to uppercase and lowercase letters.
4. When setting up the network for Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K, please operate near the router.
5. Restart your home router.
6. Please turn the Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K off and on again, restart the SwitchBot app, and then try once more following the app's guidance.
7. If you have another smartphone, please try adding Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K using that smartphone.
8. Please try connecting your Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K to your smartphone's tethering.

Regarding this issue, could you please check the following points?

### Troubleshooting: Failed to Update SwitchBot Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K Firmware

Thank you for reaching out to us. We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused and appreciate the opportunity to assist you.
Regarding the firmware update issue, could you please check the following points?
1. Please use the latest version of the SwitchBot app from the app store. Issues may occur when using the Beta version. Thank you for your understanding.
2. Perform the firmware update near the device to ensure a stable connection.
3. Turn off your home router, wait about 30 seconds, then turn it back on.
4. Switch to a mobile hotspot connection and attempt the update again.
5. If you have another smartphone, please try using it instead.
6. There may be failures due to network connection issues, so please wait for some time and try multiple times.
7. Enable "Automatic Upgrades" by going to SwitchBot app → Outdoor Pan Tilt Cam 3K → Gear icon → Firmware Version, wait for about a week, and check if the update occurred automatically.
