### Can the Photo Displayed Be Changed by Touching SwitchBot Al Art Frame?

The AI Art Frame uses E-Ink Spectra 6 technology, which is suited for static displays. Adding a touch layer would affect the display quality, so the product is designed without touch functionality. As a result, images on the AI Art Frame cannot be changed through touch.


---
### What Image File Formats Does SwitchBot AI Art Frame Support?

SwitchBot AI Art Frame supports popular image file formats, including JPEG (JPG) and PNG. In the SwitchBot app, compatible images are automatically selected for display.


---
### Does SwitchBot AI Art Frame Support Automatic Switching Between Horizontal and Vertical Screen Display for Images?

No, the AI Art Frame does not support automatic switching between landscape and portrait modes. Therefore, when you place the AI Art Frame horizontally or vertically, the image will not switch automatically. If needed, please set it to landscape or portrait mode in the SwitchBot app.


---
### Can I Play Videos on SwitchBot Al Art Frame?

No, the AI Art Frame is designed solely for displaying images and does not support video playback.


---
### Will the Image Displayed on SwitchBot AI Art Frame Disappear if the Wi-Fi Connection Is Lost?

No, the AI Art Frame will continue displaying the last set image exactly as it was, even if the Wi-Fi connection is lost or the battery runs out. The image will not disappear.

### Can I Use One Smartphone to Control Multiple SwitchBot AI Art Frames?

Yes, you can add multiple* AI Art Frames to the SwitchBot app and manage them all conveniently from a single smartphone.

### Will SwitchBot AI Art Frame Update Immediately After Changing the Image?

The AI Art Frame utilizes a display powered by E-Ink Spectra 6 technology. Due to its design, it does not support real-time image display. Once an image is selected in the SwitchBot app, it takes about one minute to render and appear on the screen. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and kindly ask for your understanding.

### How to Add SwitchBot AI Art Frame to SwitchBot App

1. Make sure your SwitchBot App version is up to date.

2. Please turn on the AI Art Frame.

3. Add your AI Art Frame to SwitchBot App in the following order:
- Launch SwitchBot App and log in.
- Tap "+" icon in the upper right corner.
- Tap "Add Device".
- Scroll down and tap "AI Art Frame" in the "Smart Home Appliances" section.
- Agree our Privacy Policy for AI Art Frame.
- Press and hold the button on the back of the device for 2 seconds to enter pairing mode.
- Tap "Connect Device" to connect the device.
- Once the connection is complete, enter the SSID and password for the 2.4GHz Wi-Fi.
- Tap "Start Using" to finish pairing.

Note:
Please use the latest version of the SwitchBot app from the app store. You may not be able to pair with the beta version.

### Troubleshooting SwitchBot AI Art Frame Won't Connect to Wi-Fi and Can't Be Added to SwitchBot App

Thank you for reaching out to us. We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused and appreciate the opportunity to assist you.
Regarding this issue, could you please check the following points?
1. Please use the latest version of the SwitchBot app from the app store. You may not be able to pair with the beta version.
2. Place the AI Art Frame closer to the Wi-Fi router (within 5 meters).  
3. Please make sure that the Bluetooth on your smartphone is enabled. If it's already on, try turning it off and then back on again.
4. If your home Wi-Fi router supports both 5GHz and 2.4GHz frequencies, please connect to the 2.4GHz network. 
5. When adding the device, make sure that you correctly enter the Wi-Fi SSID and password, including uppercase and lowercase letters.
6. Restart your home router and then try again.  
7. Press and hold the button on the back of the AI Art Frame for 15 seconds to reset it.  After resetting, attempt to reconnect your device to your home router.
8. As a troubleshooting step, check if the AI Art Frame can connect to your smartphone's hotspot.
