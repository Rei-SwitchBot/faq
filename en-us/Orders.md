We are excited that you are considering SwitchBot for your large-scale project or business needs. Thank you for choosing our technology to help make your space smarter and more efficient.

### What Should I Do If I Want to Cancel My Order?

Currently, orders placed on the SwitchBot official website cannot be canceled manually by the customer. Our team is here to assist you with your request and we appreciate your patience while we process your inquiry.

Please note that if your order has already been shipped, it may no longer be possible to cancel it. Should this happen, we recommend that you refuse the package upon delivery. Thank you for your understanding.
