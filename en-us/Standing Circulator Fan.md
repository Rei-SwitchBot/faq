### Troubleshooting: The Remote Control Falls Out of the Storage Slot on the Back When SwitchBot Standing Circulator Fan Tilts Upward.

Thank you for reaching out to us. We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused and appreciate the opportunity to assist you.
Regarding this issue, could you please check the following points?
The remote storage area on the back of the Standing Circulator Fan has a built-in magnet. When the remote control is brought close to it, it should attach magnetically and stay in place. Please check whether the magnet is working properly on your product by placing a small iron or steel item from your home against the remote storage area and seeing if it attaches.
